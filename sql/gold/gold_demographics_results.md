**Output:** results/demographics.csv

## Summary
Customer demographic distributions across gender, age cohort,
income bracket, and membership tenure. Based on 14,825 valid
customers — 2,175 invalid records excluded in Silver layer.

## Key Findings
- Customer base skews older, 45-59 and 60+ are the dominant age cohorts
- Customer base skews male, unusual for a café loyalty program
- Income is concentrated in the middle range ($40k-$80k)
- Majority of customers have under 1 year of membership tenure
  suggesting the program is relatively new or has significant
  early churn
- Most common segment: Male, 60+, $40k-$60k, under 1 year tenure
